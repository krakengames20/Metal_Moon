# Alnico - Visual Description

## Physical Appearance

**Age**: 50s
**Build**: Tall willowy build

**Skin**: Pale skin with faint shimmer (side effect of GraviMancy)

**Hair**: Silver-white hair

**Eyes**: Unnaturally bright violet eyes

**Notable Features**:
- Faint shimmer to skin from GraviMancy exposure
- Otherworldly presence
- Slight smile that never reaches eyes

## Attire

- Flowing robes with geometric patterns
- Metallic threading throughout robes
- GraviMancer guild insignia (spiral symbol)
- Mystical but practical aesthetic

## Body Language & Expression

**Expression**: Serene but unsettling. Slight smile that never reaches eyes.

**Mood**: Mystical authority figure. Speaks in cryptic pronouncements. Alien presence.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Violet and Silver Shimmer**
