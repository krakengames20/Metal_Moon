# Tanya EchoMary

Tanya is a Sergeant in New Rhodes security and Caspian Markis's trusted aide. She was awakened at age 4 and adopted by the Makis family as a maid servant, but surprised everyone by passing the Administrative exams on her first attempt.

She is married to Harold, an archivist, and has three children: Adam (16), Leo (14), and Lillian (11).

In Act 1 she is the convoy's investigator, riding in Barnabus with Marion. She photographs Alistair's boot prints, notices the deliberate foil marker, sketches the puncture pattern, and is the first to suggest Alistair "knew where to step" through the Boiling Sea — catching Garvin's reaction.
