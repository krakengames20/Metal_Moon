# Tanya EchoMary - Visual Description

## Physical Appearance

**Age**: 39 years old (awakened at age 4, pod-named Alphanumeric)
**Build**: Slender and athletic, 5'10"

**Skin**: Light brown, clear complexion

**Hair**: Red-brown, pulled back in a tight practical braid

**Eyes**: Hazel, observant, misses nothing — the kind of eyes that make people uncomfortable

**Notable Features**:
- Moves quietly and deliberately
- Often kneeling, crouching, examining — always investigating
- Carries a small sketch pad for recording evidence
- Alert stillness, like a hunting animal at rest
- Clean and precise despite the dust

## Attire

- Dark field-grade security uniform, Deployed Admin issue
- Sergeant's insignia at collar
- Duty belt with sidearm and investigation tools
- Compact field pack
- Dust scarf around neck, pulled up when needed
- Practical boots, well-maintained

## Body Language & Expression

**Expression**: Quiet, sharp, thoughtful. Speaks only when she has something worth saying. Observes details others miss.

**Mood**: The investigator who sees the pattern before anyone else names it. Patient, precise, dangerous in her own way.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Warm Umber and Steel Blue**
