# Elder Kora Dustborn

Elder Kora Dustborn is a senior TrailBlazer voice and a keeper of exile-family memory.

Her community descends from survivors of the Purge. Most of the exiled perished. Those who endured formed TrailBlazer communities and preserved the counter-narrative through oral testimony: the families were innocent, the accusations were manufactured, the children were taken as leverage, and the exile was designed to kill.

Kora is a major bridge between desert testimony and city-state investigation.
