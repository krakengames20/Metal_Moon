# Tarus Blackforge - Visual Description

## Physical Appearance

**Age**: 53 years old
**Build**: Stocky powerful build from mining

**Skin**: Dark brown skin with embedded metallic dust (occupational marking)

**Eyes**: Calculating eyes that assess value

**Notable Features**:
- Cybernetic left arm (rhodium-reinforced, industrial not sleek)
- Metallic dust embedded in skin from years of mining
- Blunt, ruthless expression

## Attire

- Miners Guild jacket with rhodium threading
- Heavy work boots
- Tool belt
- Industrial practical clothing

## Body Language & Expression

**Expression**: Calculating, blunt, ruthless pragmatism. Eyes that assess value.

**Mood**: Union boss who clawed to power. Respects strength, exploits weakness.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Industrial Iron and Forge Orange**
