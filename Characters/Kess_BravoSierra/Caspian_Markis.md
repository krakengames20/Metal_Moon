## Caspian Markis

Caspian was, like most others, born and raised in new Rhodes.  His parents were members of the administration guild, so he followed in their footsteps.  Caspian was always a big boy, strong and bossy, so it came as no surprise when he gravitated towards New Rhodes security forces.  He showed a lot of initiative and some fast thinking during his service, being it's most successful investigator.  He is currently the head of the Security services, holding the rank of Captain. He reports directly to the board of the New Rhodes Corporation and is tasked with over seeing the security forces and ensuring the security of the board members.

His aide is Sergent Tanya EchoMary, whom he trusts completely.

Caspian is in his late 40s, dark haired, lean and muscular. He stands about 6'4" tall and is clean shaven, with blue eyes.  His skills include fighting, shooting, investigating, laws and administration. He's never been married and is somewhat concerned about how much he likes Lillian FoxKilo; an affection she seems to return.  He considers his affection for her to be a potential conflict of interest.
