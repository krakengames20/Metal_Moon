## Tanya EchoMary

Tanya was 4 years old when she was awakened.  She spent 3 months in a corporate orphanage before being adopted by the Makis family with a view to raising her as a maid servant.  Over time they taught her many things, and when the restrictions on the Alphabetics were released, Tanya surprised many people by passing the Administrative exams on her first attempt and then joined the security forces after her step brother. She currently holds the rank of Sergent and is Caspians aide.

Although she could be considered Caspian Makis's step sister, he was 12 when she came into the family, already focussed on Administrative exams.  There is little affection between them, but Caspian does trust her and she works as his aid.  Their family relationship isn't widely known.

Tanya is 5'10" tall, slender and athletic. She is 39 years old. She has red/brown hair and hazel eyes.  Her skills include fighting, shooting, investigating, laws, administration and cooking.  

She is married to another Administrator, Harold, who works in archives. They have 3 children - Adam (16), Leo (14) and Lillian (11).
