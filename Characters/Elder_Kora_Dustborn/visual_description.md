# Elder Kora Dustborn - Visual Description

## Physical Appearance

**Age**: 60s
**Build**: Weathered from desert life

**Skin**: Weathered leathery skin from desert exposure

**Hair**: Steel-gray hair in braids with metal beads

**Eyes**: Piercing dark eyes, unblinking stare

**Notable Features**:
- Dust-tattoos on arms (TrailBlazer tradition)
- Desert-hardened appearance
- Intense unreadable gaze

## Attire

- Layered dust cloaks
- Reinforced desert boots
- Goggles on forehead
- Necklace of metal fragments
- Practical survivalist gear

## Body Language & Expression

**Expression**: Wise, guarded, calculating. Unreadable but intense.

**Mood**: Desert oracle, survivor, living history of TrailBlazer culture.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Desert Sand and Oxidized Copper**
