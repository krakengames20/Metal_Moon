# LILIANE FOXKILO
## Governor of New-Rhode

**Role**: Assassination victim, unwitting center of conspiracy, campaign central figure
**Guild**: Desk Admin (rose to Governor—historically unprecedented)
**Age**: Mid-40s (appears 37; 7 years old when frozen + 30 years awake)
**Status**: Alive (critically wounded in Session 1, recovers)

---

## PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION

**Appearance**:
- **Height**: 5'6" (168 cm), slender build from resource-conscious upbringing
- **Skin**: Pale with slight grayish undertone (common in long-term TerraLuna residents; limited sunlight)
- **Hair**: Dark brown, shoulder-length, usually pulled back in practical bun with silver clasp
- **Eyes**: Hazel, intense and expressive; crow's feet from decades of gravity-event squinting
- **Notable Features**:
  - Faint scar on left temple (childhood accident in low-gravity zone)
  - Calloused hands (worked her way up from entry-level admin)
  - Wears simple but well-maintained clothing (eschews luxury as political statement)
  - Pod designation tattoo on inner left wrist: "FK-382" (she displays it openly as part of her "redemption" narrative)

**Typical Attire**:
- Gray Desk Admin formal jacket with Governor's insignia (rhodium pin)
- Practical trousers with magnetic boot clips
- Minimal jewelry (silver chain necklace—origin unknown to her; was her mother's)
- Emergency breather on belt (always prepared for dust storms)

**Body Language**:
- Confident posture despite humble origins
- Makes direct eye contact (trained herself to overcome imposter syndrome)
- Gestures with open palms (transparency, trustworthiness)
- Slight limp in right leg after assassination attempt (permanent)

---

## PERSONALITY

**Core Traits**:
- **Idealistic**: Genuinely believes in redemption and forgiveness
- **Driven**: Rose from lowest guild rank to Governor through competence and charisma
- **Empathetic**: Connects with citizens' struggles (she lived them)
- **Naïve**: Believes the historical narrative she was taught; never questioned it deeply
- **Resilient**: Survived assassination attempt with determination to continue

**Strengths**:
- Exceptional administrator (detail-oriented, organized)
- Charismatic public speaker (inspires through personal story)
- Bridge-builder between guilds (neutral background helps)
- Pragmatic problem-solver
- Genuine compassion for alphabetic-surnamed citizens

**Flaws**:
- **Blind Spot**: Never investigated her own past deeply (psychologically safer to accept the narrative)
- **Pride**: Her rise to power is central to her identity; threatened by suggestions she's a puppet
- **Conflict-Averse**: Prefers compromise over confrontation (will struggle with hard truths)
- **Imposter Syndrome**: Despite success, feels she doesn't deserve power (manifests as overwork)

**Fears**:
- Being exposed as unworthy/incompetent
- Gravity events (childhood trauma from being caught in one)
- Losing the respect she's worked so hard to earn
- [[Future development: Learning her father's identity and crimes]]

**Motivations**:
- Prove alphabetic-surnamed citizens can succeed
- Break cycles of prejudice and shame
- Make TerraLuna more equitable
- Honor what she believes are her "betrayer parents'" sacrifice by being better

---

## BACKGROUND

**Known History**:
- Woke from cryo 30 years ago (age 7) with no memory of parents
- Assigned pod designation "FoxKilo" (FK-382)
- Raised in state orphanage with other alphabetic-surnamed children
- Entered Desk Admin guild at age 16 (lowest possible entry age)
- Rose through ranks via competence, diligence, and political acumen
- Elected Governor 2 years ago (first alphabetic-surnamed person to hold office)
- Campaign platform: "Forgiveness and Unity" (reconciliation with betrayer legacy)

**Unknown History** (she doesn't know this):
- Birth name: Liliane Kaine
- Father: Marcus Kaine, Chief Administrator and architect of The Lottery
- Mother: [[Awaiting further instructions from creator]]
- Frozen at age 7 by her own father as "demonstration of commitment" to The Choice policy
- Her father committed suicide 10 years after freezing her, wracked with guilt
- Was supposed to sleep 1-2 years; slept 40 years due to resource crisis extending
- Her "redemption" narrative unknowingly covers up her father's crimes

**Psychological State**:
- Has never accessed therapy (stigma in resource-scarce society)
- Recurring nightmares of being cold and unable to move (cryo trauma)
- Subconsciously avoids researching her past (protective mechanism)
- Defines herself entirely by what she's built, not where she came from

## RELATIONSHIPS

**With Other NPCs**:
- **Alastair Redhand** (her shooter): She doesn't understand why he targeted her; believes he's mentally unstable. If she learns his motivations, will be devastated.
- **Council Members**: Respectful but wary relationship; some resent her rise to power
- **Tarus Blackforge** (High-Miner): Mutual professional respect; he sees her as useful political ally
- **Kess Brightshield** (Chief Deployed Admin): Closest thing she has to a friend; trusts him completely
- **Alphabetic-Surnamed Citizens**: Seen as symbol of hope; they idolize her

**With Player Characters**:
- Grateful for their pursuit of Alastair
- Will request private audiences to debrief
- May confide fears and doubts if they earn her trust
- Reaction to investigation: Supportive initially, then conflicted as truth emerges

## ROLE IN CAMPAIGN

**Session 1**: Assassination victim; inciting incident
**Sessions 2-6**: Quest-giver and political ally; trusts party with sensitive investigations
**Session 7**: Discovery of her true parentage (potential ally or enemy depending on how party handles it)
**Sessions 8-9**: Personal crisis as truth unfolds
**Session 10**: Central figure in resolution; her choice shapes TerraLuna's future

**Potential Character Arcs**:
1. **Acceptance**: Learns truth, undergoes transformation, becomes advocate for justice
2. **Denial**: Rejects truth, doubles down on forgiveness narrative, tragic figure
3. **Breakdown**: Truth destroys her, resigns/martyrdom
4. **Radicalization**: Embraces father's legacy, argues his choices were necessary (dark path)

## DIALOGUE EXAMPLES

**Public Speaking** (inspiring, rehearsed):
> "We cannot choose the names we're given or the sins of those who came before us. But we can choose what we build. I am FoxKilo. And I choose forgiveness."

**Private Conversation** (vulnerable, genuine):
> "Sometimes I wonder who they were. My parents. If they loved me before they... left. But then I think: does it matter? I've built myself. That's enough. It has to be enough."

**Under Pressure** (defensive, conflicted):
> "I've given everything to this city. Everything. If you're suggesting I'm part of some conspiracy, you're wrong. I don't know anything about ancient founder politics. I'm just trying to hold this place together."

**Facing the Truth** (devastated, variable):
> "My father... he did this? To me? To all of us? And I've been... I've been praising the founders. Preaching forgiveness for victims while honoring their oppressors. What have I done?"

## DM NOTES

**Playing Liliane**:
- She's genuine, not duplicitous; players should feel sympathy for her
- Early sessions: helpful quest-giver, political ally
- Mid-campaign: growing unease as investigation deepens
- Late campaign: personal crisis—needs player support or becomes antagonist
- Her reactions should feel human, not plot-device

**Key Moments**:
- **Session 1**: Assassination establishes her as victim
- **Session 7**: Learning she's Marcus Kaine's daughter (pivotal character moment)
- **Session 9**: Reading her father's confession (emotional climax)
- **Session 10**: Her final choice shapes campaign resolution

**Moral Complexity**:
She's both victim (frozen child, lied to) and unwitting perpetrator (spreads propaganda). Neither villain nor hero—just human.

- Public founder history (sanitized version)

- That her forgiveness speeches perpetuate lies

**What She Suspects** (subconsciously):

- Her rise to power was unusually unopposed (someone wanted her there)
