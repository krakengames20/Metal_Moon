# Liliane FoxKilo

Liliane FoxKilo is the elected governor of New-Rhode and the target of the opening assassination attempt.

Her pod lineage is FK382. She is a former Pupil of New-Rhode — a descendant of families condemned during the Purge. She rose through the administrative system to become the colony's most visible pod-named political figure.

Liliane fully internalized the official history. She is a proud advocate for the colony's founding principles and treats her own family's condemnation as proof of New-Rhode's capacity for redemption. In public, she positions herself as living evidence that the system works — that even the children of traitors can earn the colony's trust and rise to its highest office.

She does not know the treason was fabricated. Her advocacy perpetuates the lie from the position of its most visible victim.

As governor and council chairwoman, she has high-tier access to classified archive material. Critics argue that even if she did not create the secrecy regime, she now represents it.

Some factions suspect her rise was tolerated, guided, or stage-managed by legacy power brokers to diffuse social pressure. This remains unproven in public record.

## Secrets / Hidden Knowledge

- **Classified Archive Exposure**: Hidden from the public. Liliane can access records most citizens never see.
- **Relationship to KOR Influence**: Unknown in early chapters. It is intentionally ambiguous whether she is complicit, constrained, or being used.
- **Election Legitimacy Question**: Hidden political layer. Suspicions of managed elections should surface gradually through witness testimony and records.
- **Unwitting Instrument**: Liliane's pro-colony rhetoric makes her the most effective propagandist for the Purge's false narrative — precisely because she believes it and speaks from personal conviction rather than institutional obligation.
