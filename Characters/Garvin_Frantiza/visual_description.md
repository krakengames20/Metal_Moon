# Garvin Frantiza - Visual Description

## Physical Appearance

**Age**: Early-to-mid 30s
**Build**: Tall and lean, rangy desert frame — 6'6"

**Skin**: Sun-weathered but still young, faint lines around eyes from squinting into dust storms. Metallic dust embedded in the creases of his hands and neck from years of solo mining.

**Hair**: Black, unkempt, falls past ears. Scraggly dark beard or heavy stubble

**Eyes**: Indeterminate grey, watchful, always reading the horizon

**Notable Features**:
- Perpetual cigarette or cigarette stub
- Hands rough and cracked from mining and desert work, metallic dust ground into the skin
- Moves with an unhurried economy, no wasted motion
- Tall enough to loom over everyone in the convoy
- Younger than he looks — the desert ages you fast

## Attire

- Faded Miners Guild jacket, rhodium threading still visible but dulled by dust and wear — worn open over layered work clothing
- Rhodium-plated shoulder pauldron on one side — industrial mining protection from rockfall, scratched and dented
- Acid-resistant metal forearm guards — reinforced gauntlets for handling corrosive ore and acid runoff
- Radiation dosimeter badge on chest — changes color with exposure levels
- Heavy mining work boots, steel-reinforced with metal plating over the top of each foot, caked with metallic dust
- Industrial practical clothing underneath, nothing clean, nothing decorative
- No hat — uses a dust hood pulled from his jacket collar when needed
- Guild patch on shoulder, faded but present — he's a miner, not hiding it

## Gear & Tools

- **Spectrometric ore scanner** — handheld wand on belt, reads mineral composition through rock, faint orange glow when active
- **Sonic probe** — compact ultrasonic drill for fracturing rock along crystal planes, pistol-grip form factor, holstered on hip
- **Mini survey drone** — palm-sized, folded and clipped to shoulder pauldron, scouts terrain and maps ore veins
- **Seismic spikes** — slim metal rods that map subsurface deposits via vibration, several clipped to belt like ammunition
- **Sealed sample canisters** — small cylindrical tubes for ore samples, racked in a row on belt

## Body Language & Expression

**Expression**: Guarded, laconic, deliberately unhelpful. Smokes and watches. Answers in as few words as possible.

**Mood**: A man carrying secrets he can't share. Competent and dangerous in a quiet way. Frontier loner who knows more than he lets on.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Dust Grey and Forge Orange**
