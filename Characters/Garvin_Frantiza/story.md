# Garvin Frantiza

Garvin is a miner, explorer, and trader — the only real one operating out of New Rhodes. He runs a mechanized mining convoy on extended trips into the metal desert, sometimes 100km out.

He is a member of the Frantiza family and a squire in the Grand Order of the Knights of New Rhodes. He knows about the TrailBlazers, trades with them, and brought Alistair to New Rhodes — though he didn't know about the assassination plan.

In Act 1 he serves as the convoy's guide, riding in Katie. He subtly tries to delay pursuit of Alistair while appearing cooperative. He deflects questions about the foil markers and reacts when Tanya guesses Alistair knew a safe path through the Boiling Sea.
