# SYL VOIDWALKER
## Gravimancy Matron, GraviMancer Guild Leader, Council Member

**Role**: Quest-giver, calculating faction representative, wild card
**Guild**: GraviMancer (Matron—highest rank, master-level practitioner)
**Age**: Late 40s (48)
**Status**: Alive, influential

---

## PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION

**Appearance**:
- **Height**: 5'8" (173 cm), lean and agile from constant gravity manipulation training
- **Skin**: Pale with faint gravitational field shimmer (side effect of decades of GraviMancy)
- **Hair**: Silver-white (prematurely grayed from gravity exposure), kept in tight bun with metallic pins
- **Eyes**: Gray-blue with occasional flickers of darker/lighter shades (gravity field interaction with pupils)
- **Notable Features**:
  - Moves with unnatural grace (gravity-assisted balance)
  - Fingers show gravitational scarring (dark lines like Lichtenberg figures)
  - Seems to weigh nothing when she walks (barely leaves footprints)
  - Perpetually wears gravity suit under clothing (generates faint hum)
  - Slight metallic scent (ozone from gravity manipulation)

**Typical Attire**:
- Fitted GraviMancer formal suit (black with silver threading—gravity conductor weave)
- Gravity manipulation device integrated into clothing (barely visible)
- Matron insignia (floating metallic badge that orbits her shoulder—held by gravity field)
- Reinforced boots with g-dampeners
- Minimal jewelry (interferes with gravity fields)
- Always wears gloves (hides scarring, maintains professional appearance)

**Body Language**:
- Unnaturally still (energy conservation from decades of training)
- Movements are precise and calculated (no wasted motion)
- Maintains personal space (6 ft radius—habit from gravity field generation)
- Direct, unblinking stare (assesses threats)
- Rarely touches things directly (uses micro-gravity to levitate small objects)

---

## PERSONALITY

**Core Traits**:
- **Calculating**: Every decision is strategic cost-benefit analysis
- **Pragmatic**: Ideology matters less than outcomes
- **Ambitious**: Wants GraviMancers to be recognized as essential, not just useful
- **Protective**: Fiercely defensive of guild interests and members
- **Amoral**: Right and wrong are less important than effective and ineffective

**Strengths**:
- Brilliant strategic thinker (sees three-dimensional political landscape)
- Master GraviMancer (one of the best on TerraLuna)
- Patient (plays long game)
- Adaptable (shifts positions fluidly)
- Fearless (confidence in own power)

**Flaws**:
- **Ruthless**: Will sacrifice individuals for guild/personal advancement
- **Arrogant**: Believes GraviMancers are superior (intellectually and practically)
- **Manipulative**: Uses people as chess pieces
- **Cold**: Lacks empathy; sees emotions as weaknesses
- **Overconfident**: Believes she can control outcomes through planning

**Fears**:
- GraviMancers becoming obsolete (technology replacement)
- Gravity manipulation being democratized (loses guild's unique power)
- Personal weakness (age reducing her abilities)
- Gravity events she can't control

**Motivations**:
- Elevate GraviMancer Guild to equal status with Miners
- Expand gravity manipulation technology/applications
- Personal legacy as greatest Matron in guild history
- Prove GraviMancers are indispensable to TerraLuna survival

## BACKGROUND

**Early Life**:
- Born to non-guild family (rare for GraviMancer)
- Showed exceptional aptitude for gravity manipulation at age 10
- Entered GraviMancer Academy (youngest student in 30 years)
- Rose through ranks through skill and political maneuvering

**Career Path**:
- Master GraviMancer by age 28 (record-breaking)
- Developed new gravity manipulation techniques (proprietary, guild secrets)
- Elected Matron at age 40 (youngest ever)
- Expanded guild's political influence significantly
- Implemented reforms making GraviMancers more essential to city infrastructure

**Political Strategy**:
- Positioned GraviMancers as protectors during gravity events (public relations)
- Negotiated contracts making guild essential to building construction
- Forms temporary alliances with other guilds when mutually beneficial
- Ruthlessly eliminates internal guild opposition

**Connection to Conspiracy**:
- Knows fragments of Lottery history (guild records mention gravity manipulation use during enforcement)
- Doesn't care about historical justice—cares about present power dynamics
- Views revelation as potential opportunity (chaos = chance to increase guild importance)
- Will support whichever side offers best outcome for GraviMancers

## ROLE IN CAMPAIGN

**Session 1**: Quest-giver (Emergency Council); assigns mission as political necessity

**Sessions 2-6**: Strategic player:
- Provides resources if it serves guild
- Gathers intelligence on all parties
- Positions guild for maximum advantage regardless of outcome
- May secretly support or oppose investigation based on calculations

**Sessions 7-10**: Wildcard faction:
- **If truth benefits guild**: Supports revelation (e.g., if it weakens Miners)
- **If truth threatens guild**: Opposes revelation (e.g., if GraviMancers were enforcers)
- **If chaos ensues**: Positions GraviMancers as stabilizing force (increases power)

**Final Revelation** (potential):
GraviMancer Guild records show they were used to enforce The Choice (gravity manipulation to prevent resistance, capture fleeing parents). Syl knows this. How she handles it shapes her arc.

## DIALOGUE EXAMPLES

**Mission Briefing** (cold, strategic):
> "The attempted assassination creates instability. Instability threatens infrastructure. Infrastructure failures cause gravity event casualties. This impacts everyone. You will retrieve Alastair Redhand. The GraviMancer Guild provides support because it serves our interests. Don't mistake pragmatism for altruism."

**On Politics** (calculating):
> "Emotion clouds judgment. The Governor was shot. Unfortunate. But what matters is maintaining operational stability. Find the shooter, resolve the crisis, restore function. Everything else is sentiment."

**Negotiating** (transactional):
> "You want guild assistance? Here's the exchange rate: information. Everything you learn about Alastair's investigation, you share. In return, gravity manipulation support, access to restricted zones, personal protection. Fair trade."

**On Historical Truth** (dismissive):
> "Seventy-year-old crimes? Ancient founders' choices? Irrelevant. The past is immutable. Present power dynamics are not. I care about the latter."

**Revealing Guild Involvement** (if cornered, Session 8+):
> "Yes. GraviMancers enforced The Choice. We prevented escapes, subdued resisters. We followed lawful orders. Would you prefer we'd let chaos consume the colony? Survival required hard choices. We made them. Judge if you wish. We endured."

**Final Position** (Session 10, cold logic):
> "Truth or lies, stability or chaos—I choose whichever strengthens the GraviMancer Guild. You find that amoral? Perhaps. But when the next crisis comes, when gravity inverts and buildings fail, you'll be grateful we're powerful enough to save you. Power serves survival. Everything else is luxury."

## STAT BLOCK

**Syl Voidwalker** (Gravimancy Matron, Level 7)
*Medium humanoid (human), Lawful Neutral*

**AC** 16 (gravity suit + gravity manipulation defense)
**HP** 52 (7d8+14)
**Speed** 30 ft (fly 30 ft with gravity manipulation)

**STR** 10 (+0), **DEX** 16 (+3), **CON** 14 (+2)
**INT** 18 (+4), **WIS** 15 (+2), **CHA** 14 (+2)

**Skills**: Arcana +8, Investigation +8, Perception +6, Intimidation +6
**Saving Throws**: INT +8, WIS +6
**Tools**: Gravity manipulation device

**Special Abilities**:
- **Master GraviMancer**: Can manipulate gravity in 30 ft radius
- **Gravity Field**: Reaction to impose disadvantage on attacks against her (3 uses, recharge on short rest)
- **Weightless Movement**: Can fly/hover using gravity manipulation
- **Crush**: Bonus action to increase gravity on target (STR save DC 16 or restrained)
- **Guild Authority**: Can requisition GraviMancer support

**Actions**:
- **Gravity Lance**: Ranged spell attack +8 to hit, range 60 ft, 3d8 force damage
- **Gravitational Crush**: Target in 30 ft makes STR save DC 16 or takes 4d6 force damage and is restrained until end of Syl's next turn
- **Inversion Field** (recharge 5-6): 20 ft radius sphere, all creatures make DEX save DC 16 or be thrown upward 30 ft and take falling damage

**Gravity Manipulation** (out of combat):
- Levitate objects up to 200 lbs
- Create localized gravity zones (low-g or high-g)
- Stabilize structures during gravity events
- Detect gravitational anomalies within 1 mile

**Combat Tactics**:
- Fights from distance (ranged attacks)
- Uses gravity manipulation for battlefield control
- Restrains threats, then eliminates systematically
- Retreats if outmatched (no false bravery)

**What GraviMancers Did During The Lottery**:
1. Used gravity manipulation to prevent escapes (inverted gravity to pin fleeing parents)
2. Subdued resistance during child removals (crush fields)
3. Assisted in executions (gravity crush—clean, efficient)
4. Created gravity barriers around Cryo Facility (prevented rescue attempts)

**Syl's Knowledge**:
- Knows guild's full involvement (has access to sealed records)
- Knows several current GraviMancers are descendants of enforcers
- Knows guild was paid in resource priority (priority water/oxygen allocation)

**Her Vulnerability**:
If guild's enforcement role is exposed, GraviMancers could be targeted for retribution. She'll act ruthlessly to prevent this—including silencing witnesses, destroying evidence, or striking deals.
