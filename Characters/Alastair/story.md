# Alastair

Alastair is a former Deployed Admin who attacked Governor Liliane FoxKilo during a public address.

He uncovered evidence that the Purge-era treason charges were fabricated — that KOR manufactured the accusations to consolidate power during the founding crisis. His findings include revision logs showing deliberate alteration of founder-era records.

What radicalized him was not the historical crime alone but its present-day perpetuation. Liliane FoxKilo, a descendant of the purged families, had become the colony's most prominent voice defending the very system that destroyed her own lineage — repeating the lie without knowing it was one. For Alastair, watching a victim champion her own victimization was intolerable.

Within New-Rhode, he is treated as both criminal and destabilizing witness.

## Secrets / Hidden Knowledge

- **Purge Fabrication Evidence**: Secret until discovered at the end of the opening chapter. Alastair holds archive fragments — including revision logs — indicating that the treason charges were manufactured.
- **Active Concealment**: Hidden and contested. Alastair believes the coverup is maintained in the present by high-clearance administrators, not only preserved by institutional inertia.
- **True Motive Behind the Attack**: Hidden from most characters early in the campaign. The public narrative frames him as a terrorist. His actual aim is forced disclosure.
- **The Ruse**: Unconfirmed. The assassination attempt may have been deliberately designed to fail — a provocation intended to draw a pursuit team outside the colony, where Alastair could present his evidence to witnesses beyond KOR's reach.
