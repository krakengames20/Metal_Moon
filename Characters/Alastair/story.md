# Alastair

Alastair is a former Deployed Admin who attempted to assassinate Governor Liliane FoxKilo.

He uncovered evidence that founder-era history was manipulated and that forced cryostasis was used against families during the early colony split.

His motive was not only revenge for past crimes. He believed a small high-clearance admin circle was still suppressing truth in the present, and that Liliane had become the public face of that concealment.

Within New-Rhode, Alastair is treated as both criminal and destabilizing witness. He is tied directly to the opening manhunt and to the buried record conflict at the center of the campaign.

## Secrets / Hidden Knowledge

- **Founder-Era Record Manipulation Evidence**: Secret until discovered at the end of the opening chapter. Alastair holds fragments indicating that official founder history was deliberately rewritten.
- **Active Secrecy by High-Clearance Admins**: Hidden and contested. Alastair believes concealment is ongoing in the present day, not only historical.
- **True Motive Behind the Attack**: Hidden from most characters early on. Public narrative frames him as a terrorist; his internal motive is forced disclosure.
