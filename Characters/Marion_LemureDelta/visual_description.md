# Marion LemureDelta - Visual Description

## Physical Appearance

**Age**: Mid-30s (awakened at age 11, pod-named Alphanumeric)
**Build**: Short but sturdy, compact and strong from years of physical engineering work

**Skin**: Olive-tanned, grease-smudged, calloused hands permanently stained with machine oil

**Hair**: Dark brown, chopped short and practical, often pushed back with welding goggles

**Eyes**: Sharp dark brown eyes, always assessing, always noticing mechanical details

**Notable Features**:
- Grease permanently under fingernails
- Burn scars on forearms from soldering and welding
- Tool belt always on her hip
- Slight squint from years of close-up precision work
- Compact muscular frame, low center of gravity

## Attire

- Heavy-duty engineer's overalls, patched and re-patched, pockets bulging with tools
- Welding goggles pushed up on forehead or around neck
- Steel-toed boots, scuffed and acid-scarred
- Leather tool belt with wrenches, pliers, wire cutters
- Fingerless gloves, worn through at the palms

## Body Language & Expression

**Expression**: Wry, skeptical half-smile. Eyes constantly scanning for what's broken. More comfortable with machines than people.

**Mood**: Practical sardonic warmth. Trusts metal more than men. Dry humor masking sharp intelligence.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Engine Oil Black and Tarnished Brass**
