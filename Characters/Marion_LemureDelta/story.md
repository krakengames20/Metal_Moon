# Marion LemureDelta

Marion is an engineer, tinkerer, and reparator of all odd things mechanical.

She was awakened at age 11 with her memory wiped but practical skills intact. She engineered several escapes from the corporate orphanage before being adopted by Laurance Hollander, a senior engineer. She aced the engineering guild exams and became an expert mechanic and fabricator.

She owns and maintains Barnabus, an old Mule she has constantly tinkered with and adapted over the years. She prefers machines over people, is single, and frankly prefers a wrench to a conversation.

She is friendly with Garvin but distrusts him — she'll travel and sleep with him but doesn't quite trust him. She doesn't know about the Trail Blazers or the KOR.

In Act 1 she serves as the convoy's chief mechanic, riding in Barnabus with Tanya. She is the first to notice Garvin's evasions and quietly flags them to Tanya.
