## Garvin Frantiza

Garvan is a miner, explorer and trader, the only real one operating out of the New Rhodes colony.  He has a mechanised mining convoy with which he makes extended trips into the metal desert and broken lands around New Rhodes, sometimes travelling as far as 100km away from the colony.  He's fairly practical and crude, preferring his own company to that of others.  He's found a few unique mining sites and has left autonomous mines in place atop them; when he visits them he collects the ore they have uncovered.

Garvan is a man of few words and many secrets:
    • He is a member of the Frantiza family, and a squire in Grand Order of the Knights of New Rhodes.
    • He knows about the TrailBlazers and is known to them. He often breaks his trips at their camp, trading food and equipment from new Rhodes with them.  The Knights are aware of this, but not the general populace. The knights get first refusal of anything interesting he brings back, and he also does some trades specifically for them (especially Tongral, the spirit they distil from native plants).
    • He knows Alistair and gave him a life to new Rhodes; although he didn't know Alistair was going to try and assassinate Lillian.  He'll bring a few TrailBlazers into the camp each year, his caravan being the most reliable transport across the metal desert.

Garvan is tall and skinny, maybe 6'6" with dirty blonde hair and usually stubble or a scraggly beard.  He takes little pride in his dress, focusing on practical, if worn or dirty, items.  Out in the metal desert he's more focussed on survival than appearances.  His eyes are an indeterminate grey.  He has few friends and is closer to his mechas and drones than to any people.

He'll try and delay them following the Asassins trail (hoping it'll degrade over time) but is eventaully forced to lead them into the TrailBlazer camp.
