# Metal Moon - Visual Style Guide

## Core Art Style: Sgraffito Industrial Expressionism

### Technique
**Sgraffito** is a technique where thick layers of paint are applied and then scraped away to reveal different colors and textures underneath. For Metal Moon, this translates into:

- Thick impasto paint layers scraped away to reveal metallic textures underneath
- Bold, confident brushstrokes with aggressive energy
- High contrast graphic quality that demands attention
- Strong silhouettes that read clearly even at distance
- Intentional texture that reinforces the metallic, industrial world

### Aesthetic Principles

1. **Strong Silhouettes**: Characters and locations should be recognizable from their outline alone
2. **Intentional Texture**: Every scraped mark, every brushstroke tells the story of TerraLuna's harsh metal environment
3. **Less Visual Noise**: Simplified forms that focus on essential details, avoiding unnecessary decoration
4. **Graphic Quality**: High contrast between light and dark, between smooth and textured

### Color Palette

**Base Colors:**
- Industrial grays (multiple values from charcoal to light steel)
- Deep shadows (near-black, no atmospheric softening)
- Metallic undertones (rhodium silver, iron gray, titanium white)

### Texture Vocabulary

**Surface Treatments:**
- Scratched metal (fine parallel lines revealing substrate)
- Chipped paint (irregular edges exposing metal beneath)
- Crystalline fractures (geometric break patterns)
- Oxidized surfaces (rust blooms, verdigris)
- Widmanstätten patterns (cross-hatched meteoritic metal etching)
- Pallasite olivine (amber crystalline inclusions)

### Lighting Approach

**No Atmospheric Diffusion:**
TerraLuna has no atmosphere to scatter light, resulting in:
- Stark, hard-edged shadows
- No gradient between light and dark
- Black sky even during "day"
- Stars visible always
- Extreme contrast between illuminated and shadowed areas

**Light Sources:**
- Harsh artificial lighting (cold, clinical)
- Olivine crystal glow (warm amber, organic)
- Fire (impossibly rare and precious)
- Gas giant reflection (dramatic, otherworldly)

### Character-Specific Rendering

#### Reference Images (Clean Base)
**Purpose**: Derivatives, VTT tokens, character identification
**Treatment:**
- Softer sgraffito treatment - texture present but not obscuring
- Even lighting reveals facial details, scars, expressions clearly
- Front-facing centered bust portrait
- Direct eye contact with viewer
- Character distinctly separated from background
- Features clearly readable for easy identification

#### Dramatic Images (Full Style)
**Purpose**: Publication, atmospheric storytelling, emotional impact
**Treatment:**
- Full aggressive sgraffito technique
- Dramatic lighting with deep shadows
- Dynamic composition and viewing angles
- Character integrated with environment
- Maximum texture and expressionist energy
- Emotional atmosphere prioritized over perfect clarity

### Location-Specific Rendering

#### Worm's Eye View
**Purpose**: Scene entry, establishing shots, convey scale and oppression
**Camera:**
- Extreme low-angle shot looking upward
- 14mm ultra-wide angle lens effect
- Camera at ground level or lower

**Composition:**
- Foreground: Textured ground detail to anchor perspective
- Midground: Human-scale elements for scale reference
- Background: Towering vertical elements dominating frame
- Deep depth of field maintaining sharpness throughout

#### Cinematic View
**Purpose**: Atmospheric establishing shots, narrative context
**Camera:**
- Eye-level or slightly elevated
- 35mm standard lens (natural human perspective)
- Traditional composition with horizon at thirds

**Composition:**
- Balanced framing showing environment character
- Human figures for scale and context
- Strong silhouettes against dramatic sky
- Emphasize mood over technical accuracy

#### Tactical View
**Purpose**: Battle maps, VTT maps, tactical planning
**Camera:**
- Top-down or high isometric angle
- 85mm lens for compression and clarity
- Orthographic or near-orthographic projection

**Composition:**
- Clear spatial relationships
- Simplified detail for readability
- Grid-friendly layout
- Optimized for computer vision/semantic segmentation

### Background Treatment

#### Characters:
- Oxidized meteoric iron surface
- Subtle geometric Widmanstätten pattern etching
- Ambient warm glow from distant pallasite olivine clusters (golden-amber light)
- Dark, atmospheric
- Background does NOT obscure character
- Provides context without dominating

#### Locations:
- TerraLuna's metal substrate visible
- Black void sky with stars
- Gas giant Typhon looming on horizon when relevant
- Geometric metallic features (no organic curves)
- Harsh environmental conditions visible

### Special Visual Elements

**Metal Moon Geology:**
- Widmanstätten patterns: Cross-hatched octahedral crystal structure visible in meteoritic iron
- Pallasite olivine: Amber-green crystalline inclusions in iron matrix
- Rhodium deposits: Silver-white metallic sheen (valuable, status symbol)
- Rust blooms: Iron oxide creating orange-brown surface discoloration

**Environmental Effects:**
- Gravity anomalies: Objects suspended impossibly, dust swirling in wrong directions
- Dust storms: Metallic particles creating silver-gray veils
- Cold: Frost patterns on metal, breath clouds
- Vacuum exposure: No wind-blown hair, no atmospheric haze

### Mood and Atmosphere

**Overall Tone:** Dark, gritty, industrial, oppressive yet beautiful

**Emotional Qualities:**
- Isolation and exposure
- Resilience in hostile environment
- Beauty in harshness
- Human struggle against alien world
- Geological time vs. human lifespan
- Authority and control (in city environments)
- Freedom and danger (in desert environments)

### Technical Specifications

**Image Format:** JPEG
**Target File Size:** Under 500KB (for web distribution)
**Compression:** Quality 85, optimized
**Resolution:** Suitable for print and digital display
**Color Space:** sRGB

### Style Consistency Rules

1. **Always maintain strong silhouettes** - recognize characters from outline
2. **Texture serves story** - every scrape shows TerraLuna's harsh environment
3. **Respect the palette** - industrial grays + specific accents only
4. **No atmosphere** - remember TerraLuna has no air, lighting is stark
5. **Metal dominates** - this is a metal moon, surfaces are metallic not organic
6. **Sgraffito is signature** - the scraped, layered technique defines the aesthetic

## Usage Notes

This style guide defines the visual identity of Metal Moon. When generating new images:

1. Reference the entity's specific color accents
2. Apply appropriate rendering mode (reference vs. dramatic for characters, viewpoint template for locations)
3. Maintain sgraffito technique consistency
4. Respect the harsh, no-atmosphere lighting
5. Keep backgrounds appropriate to entity type
6. Ensure strong silhouettes and intentional texture
7. File outputs with proper naming: `{entity}_{mode}_{style}_{composition}_{index}.jpg`
