# 6 - Opening Quest: Hunt for Alastair

After the assassination attempt, a cross-guild team is assembled to track Alastair into the desert and bring him back.

The stated objective is to locate him, return him alive if possible, and determine the scope of any conspiracy behind the attack.

The pursuit becomes more than a manhunt. Alastair is both attacker and witness. His evidence points to fabricated treason charges at the foundation of New-Rhode's political order and to ongoing concealment by high-clearance administrators.

The arc moves toward founder terrain in the deep desert, including The Scar and nearby cache zones.

If Alastair's claims hold, the real antagonist shifts from a single fugitive to an active power network: the Knights of Rhodes.

## The Ruse Theory

The assassination attempt may not have been what it appeared. Alastair may have designed the attack to fail — a calculated provocation to force the colony to send a pursuit team into the desert, beyond KOR's surveillance and institutional control. Outside the walls of New-Rhode, he can present his evidence directly to witnesses who have no reason to suppress it.

If true, the manhunt is the outcome he wanted.
