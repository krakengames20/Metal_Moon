# 6 - Opening Quest: Hunt for Alastair

After the assassination attempt, a cross-guild team is assembled to track Alastair into the desert and bring him back.

The stated objective is to locate him, return him alive if possible, and secure any documents or recordings in his possession.

The pursuit becomes more than a manhunt. Alastair is both attacker and witness, and his evidence points to deliberate manipulation of founder-era history.

The arc moves toward founder terrain in the deep desert, including The Scar and nearby cache zones.
