# 6 - Opening Quest: Hunt for Alastair

After the assassination attempt, a cross-guild team is assembled to track Alastair into the desert and bring him back.

The stated objective is to locate him, return him alive if possible, and secure any documents or recordings in his possession.

The pursuit becomes more than a manhunt. Alastair is both attacker and witness, and his evidence points to deliberate manipulation of founder-era history and continued secrecy by high-clearance admins.

The arc moves toward founder terrain in the deep desert, including The Scar and nearby cache zones.

If Alastair's claims hold, the real antagonist shifts from a single fugitive to an active power network: the Knights of Rhodes (KOR).

## Secrets / Hidden Knowledge Track

- **Secret A: Founder-Era History Was Manipulated**
Reveal target: end of the opening chapter.
- **Secret B: Concealment Continues in the Present**
Reveal target: mid investigation, after first recovered archive fragment.
- **Secret C: KOR as Active Antagonist Network**
Reveal target: late opening arc, once family-link evidence is corroborated.
