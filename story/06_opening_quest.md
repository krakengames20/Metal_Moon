# V — OPENING QUEST: The Hunt for Alastair

## The Inciting Event

Governor **Liliane FoxKilo** — the first citizen with an alphanumeric pod-name surname to hold political office in New-Rhode — is **shot** during a public address. The shooter is identified as **Alastair Redhand**, a former Deployed Admin who has gone rogue.

Before she is shot, Liliane is giving a speech that touches on the history of the pod-named citizens — their origins, their place in New-Rhode's society, the taboo that surrounds them. It is a brave and politically dangerous speech. It is interrupted by a gunshot.

Alastair flees into the dust desert.

---

## The Party

Three guilds each send one representative to pursue Alastair and bring him back for charter proceedings. A TrailBlazer is hired to guide them across the wasteland. The party:

1. **A Miner** — representing the Miners' Guild. Powerful, practical, politically motivated.
2. **A Deployed Admin** — representing law enforcement. Tasked with lawful apprehension.
3. **A GraviMancer** — representing the GraviMancy Guild. Combat capability and environmental navigation.
4. **A TrailBlazer** — hired for credits. Knows the desert. Has their own reasons for everything they do. *At least for now.*

This multi-guild composition is standard practice: when an event rises to city-wide significance — such as an attempt on the Governor's life — influential guilds designate one representative each to the investigation. No single guild can dominate the proceedings.

---

## The Mission

Find Alastair. Bring him back alive for charter justice.

Cross the dust desert. Navigate gravity anomalies, Rustie territories, and the political minefield of what Alastair knows — and why he did what he did.

He is heading toward **The Scar**.

---

## What They Don't Know Yet

- Alastair's attack was motivated by a belief that Liliane is complicit in covering up the foundational event (The Lottery / The Choice).
- He is right about the conspiracy. He is wrong about Liliane's involvement.
- Liliane is herself one of the frozen children — and the daughter of the man who designed the policy.
- The truth, when it surfaces, will be more complicated than anyone expects.
