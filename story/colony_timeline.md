# Colony Timeline

Note: TerraLuna chronology below uses relative colony years (CY) because multiple records conflict. See 04_foundational_event.md for full detail on the Purge.

## CY 0 - Landing and Extraction Start
- Founders establish first industrial habitats on TerraLuna.
- Initial charter framework links governance to extraction output.

## CY 1-5 - Expansion Phase
- Settlement infrastructure stabilizes around metal production.
- Guild structures form to manage labor, safety, and enforcement.

## Early Founding Crisis (exact year disputed)
- Founder bloc fractures into Leavers and Remainers.
- Leavers depart unexpectedly with resources the colony cannot afford to lose.
- Remainers frame the departure as selfish betrayal. Resource shortfall follows.

## The Purge
- Remainer leadership (proto-KOR) fabricates treason charges against ~5% of remaining families — the least productive households, many with relatives among the Leavers.
- Accused families are condemned and exiled. Their children are seized, declared Pupils of New-Rhode, and placed in cryostasis.
- Children's family names are stripped and replaced with cryo pod identifiers.
- Cryostasis functions as a hostage mechanism against the exiled.
- Most exiled families perish in the desert. Survivors form TrailBlazer communities.

## Long Stasis Era
- Children remain in cryostasis for decades.
- The Purge hardens into accepted public history: traitors were justly expelled, their children were mercifully saved.
- KOR consolidates as the Knights of Rhodes and embeds itself in the governance structure.
- Founder-era records are altered. Cryo facility access is restricted to KOR-affiliated administrators.

## Staggered Thaw Period
- Children are thawed in small groups over years.
- Each child is raised inside the official narrative and given a pod-ID name as their legal identity.
- Pod-named citizens enter colony life under stigma tied to their families' supposed treason.

## Legacy Network Consolidation (date uncertain)
- KOR persists across generations through selective archive access, managed appointments, and intermediary influence.

## Recent Political Shift
- Liliane FoxKilo (pod lineage FK382) rises through administration.
- She becomes elected governor of New-Rhode and council chairwoman.
- She publicly cites her own family's condemnation as evidence of New-Rhode's capacity for clemency and renewal.
- Debate continues over whether her rise was a genuine breakthrough, a managed concession, or both.

## Present Day Crisis
- Alastair uncovers evidence that the Purge-era treason charges were fabricated.
- He concludes that the coverup remains actively maintained by high-clearance administrators.
- During a public address, he attacks Liliane — though his true motive may be to lure a pursuit team outside the colony where he can present his evidence without KOR interference.
- Cross-guild pursuit begins. The colony enters political rupture.

## Current External Pressure
- A newly arrived colony ship reaches TerraLuna orbit.
- Existing city-states prepare for contract competition, claims disputes, and potential alliance shifts.
