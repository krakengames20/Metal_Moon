# Colony Timeline

Note: TerraLuna chronology below uses relative colony years (CY) because multiple records conflict.

## CY 0 - Landing and Extraction Start
- Founders establish first industrial habitats on TerraLuna.
- Initial charter framework links governance to extraction output.

## CY 1-5 - Expansion Phase
- Settlement infrastructure stabilizes around metal production.
- Guild structures form to manage labor, safety, and enforcement.

## Early Founding Crisis (exact year disputed)
- Founder bloc fractures into Leavers and Remainers.
- Leavers depart with resources they claim as their share.
- Remainers characterize departure as betrayal.

## Separation Period
- Families linked to leavers face coercive policy: desert exile or child cryostasis.
- Children are placed into cryostasis by force in many cases.
- Family names are removed from records and replaced with pod-ID based names.

## Long Stasis Era
- Children remain in cryostasis for decades.
- Public archive narrative hardens around the "traitor" framing.
- Discussion of the event becomes taboo.

## Post-Stasis Integration Era
- Pod-named citizens are reintroduced into city life under stigma.
- Their social mobility remains constrained by archive-era prejudice.

## Recent Political Shift
- Liliane FoxKilo (pod ID FK382 lineage) rises through administration.
- She becomes elected head of New-Rhode.

## Present Day Crisis
- Alastair uncovers evidence challenging official founder history.
- During a public address, he attempts to assassinate Liliane.
- Cross-guild pursuit begins, and the colony enters narrative and political rupture.
