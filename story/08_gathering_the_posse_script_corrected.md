GATHERING THE POSSE

TEASER

EXT. AGORA SQUARE - NEW-RHODE - DAY

Acid-rated canopies span the square. Drainage pipes carry corrosive runoff into sealed gutters.

A temporary stage faces the ADMINISTRATIVE HUB. A hand-painted banner reads: TOGETHER WE BUILD.

A dense crowd: miners, desk admins, families, security.

At the podium, GOVERNOR LILIANE FOXKILO (40s, composed, exhausted) addresses the city.

*Liliane steadies both hands on the podium, eyes moving from the front rows to the outer crowd lanes.*

                              LILIANE
            You have heard the warnings. Lower yields.
            Delayed shipments. More waiting.

*She leans in, voice firming as she pivots from fear to policy.*

                              LILIANE (CONT'D)
            Here is the plan. West-side reclamation in
            sixty days. Second diagnostic rig this
            quarter. Education block expansion starts
            this cycle.

THWACK.

A bolt strikes her high in the chest. She collapses behind the podium.

The crowd freezes, then erupts.

CHIEF DEPLOYED ADMIN KESS BRAVOSIERRA (40s, controlled under pressure) sprints in, drops to a knee, checks pulse.

SERGEANT TANYA arrives with two officers.

*Tanya's eyes snap to the roofline and upper windows before she speaks.*

                              TANYA
            No second shot.

*Kess keeps one hand on Liliane's shoulder while scanning over Tanya's line of sight.*

                              KESS
            Perimeter. Eyes up.

Medics rush in.

*One medic points at the bolt track while the second readies the lift board.*

                              MEDIC
            Bolt is seated. No exit wound. We move now.

Kess scans the far side of the square. On COLOSSUS TOWER, a flicker of movement on the thirteenth-floor ledge.

*He locks onto the ledge and signals with a sharp two-finger point.*

                              KESS
            Ledge. Thirteen.

*Tanya tracks the point line, confirms, then pivots toward the nearest route through the crowd.*

                              TANYA
            Move.

They push through the crowd as medics carry Liliane into the Hub.

CUT TO:

INT./EXT. COLOSSUS TOWER - 13TH FLOOR SERVICE LEDGE - CONTINUOUS

Kess and Tanya step out onto a narrow exterior grate.

No shooter.

Kess kneels and points to bright metallic grains trapped in rusted mesh.

*Tanya crouches beside him, following the grain pattern from center to drain edge.*

                              KESS
            See that?

*Without looking up, Tanya rubs a sample between glove fingers and checks residue color.*

                              TANYA
            Exterior metal dust. Fresh.

Kess taps his communicator.

*He keeps his body angled toward the hatch while speaking, covering both ledge and exit point.*

                              KESS
            Control, BravoSierra. Pull all Colossus
            feeds. Thirteenth floor through freight core.

A SECURITY OPERATOR answers over static.

                              SECURITY OPERATOR (V.O.)
            Copy... One shooter. Crossbow setup.
            Entered by service stair. Exited east
            freight lift.

*Kess shifts his gaze to Tanya, then back to the hatch camera blind spot.*

                              KESS
            ID.

                              SECURITY OPERATOR (V.O.)
            Partial match: Alastair. Former Deployed
            Admin.

*Tanya's jaw tightens; she keeps her stance low and stable on the grate.*

                              KESS
            Exit route?

                              SECURITY OPERATOR (V.O.)
            Third-floor service corridor. Heading west
            logistics lanes.

                              KESS
            Lock what routes you can. Push the clip.

CUT TO:

EXT. WESTERN LOGISTICS GATE - NEW-RHODE PERIMETER - AFTERNOON

Kess and Tanya exit in storm coats, eye shields, and filter masks.

Outside air is breathable but sulfuric and wet. Metallic grit skates across concrete.

A clean footprint trail heads northwest into badlands.

*Tanya follows the print spacing and heel depth before speaking.*

                              TANYA
            Straight line. He knew where he was going.

*Kess studies the same line, then the horizon beyond grid coverage.*

                              KESS
            We do not chase blind.

He radios operations.

                              KESS (CONT'D)
            Priority pursuit. Rover package, scout
            drones, long-range hazard kit.

*Tanya keeps eyes on the trail while setting her field kit on one knee.*

                              TANYA
            We need a guide.

                              KESS
            Yes.

Tanya casts a print and tags bearing.

*She checks compass twice, then seals the sample bag and labels it against timestamp.*

                              TANYA
            Heading two-nine-four. Sample bagged.

Kess watches the trail vanish into broken terrain.

END TEASER.

ACT ONE

INT. MINERS' GUILD - NEW-RHODE - EVENING

Shift horns fade in the distance.

TARUS (50s, hard-edged union power broker) meets them on the floor.

*Tarus searches Kess's face first, looking for outcome before details.*

                              TARUS
            How is she?

                              KESS
            Alive. Critical.

*Tarus exhales once, then squares his shoulders back into problem-solving mode.*

                              TARUS
            What do you need?

                              KESS
            Shooter went west and out. We need rover
            support, drones, and a deep-route guide.

*Tarus glances toward the route board on the wall, mentally scanning who can survive far-range lanes.*

                              TARUS
            Most crews stay near survey grids. One
            exception: Gavin Peterson. He runs far and
            comes back with ore nobody else finds.

                              KESS
            Where?

                              TARUS
            Northern Mining Lock.

Tarus hands over a black AUTH TOKEN.

*He presses the token into Kess's palm rather than setting it down, making the request personal.*

                              TARUS (CONT'D)
            Show him this. Guild priority.

(beat)

*Tarus holds eye contact, voice lower and harder.*

                              TARUS (CONT'D)
            Find who did this.

                              KESS
            We will.

As Kess turns to leave:

                              TARUS
            If trust in records breaks, the city breaks.

CUT TO:

INT. NORTHERN MINING LOCK - NEW-RHODE - NIGHT

A vast industrial hall alive with prep: rover bays, drone racks, cargo skids.

Near a burn barrel, GAVIN PETERSON (40s, rangy, desert-weathered) stands as they approach.

*Gavin clocks their uniforms, then the pace of their approach, reading urgency before rank.*

                              GAVIN
            You look official.

                              KESS
            Chief Deployed Admin Kess BravoSierra.
            Sergeant Tanya. We need Gavin
            Peterson.

                              GAVIN
            That's me.

Kess offers Tarus's token. Gavin scans it, pockets it.

*Gavin's expression shifts from casual to operational as the authorization resolves.*

                              GAVIN (CONT'D)
            Route?

                              KESS
            West gate exit. Track heading two-nine-four.
            We move at first light.

*Gavin points toward the bay doors and the dark beyond them.*

                              GAVIN
            Then you follow my calls outside. Every time.

                              KESS
            Agreed.

*Gavin taps a rover panel scarred by field repairs.*

                              GAVIN
            Bring an engineer. Gravity faults and
            corrosion failures will stop you fast.

*Tanya steps half a pace forward, focused and exact.*

                              TANYA
            Recommendation?

                              GAVIN
            Marion LemurDelta. Engineers' Guild.

                              KESS
            0700 departure.

                              GAVIN
            We'll be ready.

CUT TO:

INT. ENGINEERS' GUILD - NEW-RHODE - NIGHT

A quiet lobby. One night CLERK wakes as Kess hits the bell.

                              CLERK
            What do you need?

                              KESS
            Master engineer. 0700. Northern Mining Lock.

*The clerk straightens, then stalls behind procedure.*

                              CLERK
            Outside assignment needs review windows and--

                              MARION (O.S.)
            Go back to sleep, Thomas.

MISTRESS MARION LEMURDELTA (50s, compact, grease-stained, sharp eyes) steps in from a rear corridor.

*Marion wipes one hand on a shop rag, assessing both officers in a single glance.*

                              MARION
            You said outside.

                              KESS
            Assassination pursuit. Shooter is Alastair.
            Deep-route chase.

*At Alastair's name, Marion's expression sharpens, interest replacing annoyance.*

                              MARION
            Gavin involved?

                              KESS
            Yes.

                              MARION
            Fifty percent over standard field rate.

                              KESS
            Approved.

                              MARION
            Then I will be there at 0700.

Kess and Tanya head out.

*Tanya keeps her voice low once they clear the desk area.*

                              TANYA
            Do we trust her?

*Kess does not slow, eyes fixed down-corridor toward the exit.*

                              KESS
            We trust competence.

CUT TO:

TAG

INT. ADMINISTRATIVE HUB - SECURE BRIEFING ROOM - LATE NIGHT

Tanya uploads tower footage. Kess reviews frame captures.

Freeze-frame: ALASTAIR on the ledge, slate strapped to his forearm.

Image enhancement isolates text on the slate:

FOUNDER SEPARATION LEDGER - REVISION LOG

A red clearance stamp beneath it:

RESTRICTED: CHAIR + TIER-ALPHA ADMIN

*Tanya leans closer to the display, eyes moving from the title to the clearance mark.*

                              TANYA
            He was trying to expose something, not just
            kill her.

*Kess holds the frame a beat, then closes the file with deliberate control.*

                              KESS
            Yes.

                              KESS (CONT'D)
            0700. We bring him back alive.

FADE OUT.

END OF CHAPTER.
