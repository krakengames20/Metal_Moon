# 2 - Society, Guilds, and Social Lines

## Guild Function

Guilds are the functional backbone of colony life. They are labor structure, social identity, and survival network at the same time.

Core guild lanes in New-Rhode:
- Desk Admins: logistics, scheduling, permits, records
- Miners: extraction operations and heavy machinery
- GraviMancers: gravity-control specialists for safety and combat environments
- Deployed Admins: enforcement, incident response, field investigation
- TrailBlazers (informal): desert experts outside formal city governance

## Pod-Named Citizens

A visible social line runs through the colony: citizens named from cryo pod IDs (for example, FoxKilo).

These names came from a historical erasure policy after founder conflict.
Public discussion of that history is socially and politically sensitive.

## Everyday Culture

Life is practical, compressed, and high risk.
- children are raised around emergency drills and gravity protocols
- family planning is shaped by ration costs and housing limits
- social trust usually follows guild and neighborhood ties

People call this normal. That does not mean it is stable.
