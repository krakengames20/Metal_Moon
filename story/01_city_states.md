# 1 — TONE & STYLE: The City-States

## Overview

The societies of TerraLuna are organized into a set of **city-states** — between two and four active at any time. Each was established to exploit a key deposit of rare metals, and each city is built on top of large meteorite deposits that form its foundation and its identity.

---

## The City-States

### New-Rhode
Built on Rhodium deposits. The primary setting of the campaign. A city of roughly 50,000 colonists organized into guilds. Its governance is charter-based; its current Governor is Liliane FoxKilo.

### Tantalos
Built on Tantalum deposits. A rival city-state. Site of the **Tantalos War** — a conflict roughly 45 years ago that devastated New-Rhode and shaped its current political and cultural memory. Tantalos is known for aggression and expansion.

### IC (Iridium City)
Built on Iridium deposits. A smaller, more isolated city-state. Less politically active but economically significant due to Iridium's extreme acid-resistance.

### TitanTown
Built on Titanium deposits. The youngest of the city-states. Known for its engineering culture and for supplying structural materials to the other cities.

---

## Tone

The world is **gritty, mid-tech, and unequal**. Precious metals are abundant; potable water is not. Political power is wielded through guilds. Justice is administered through charters, not courts. Citizens survive through social contracts, guild membership, and hard-won trust.

Life on TerraLuna is dangerous and unglamorous — but it is *life*. The cities are alive with culture, tension, gossip, and ambition. The gravity events are terrifying but also normalized. Children giggle during them. Parents exchange knowing glances. This is home.
