# 1 - City-States

## Political Shape

TerraLuna is divided into corporate colonies that function as city-states.

Each city-state has:
- a settlement charter
- a council tied to corporate stakeholders
- an elected or appointed operational head
- local guild structures for labor, security, and logistics

## New-Rhode

New-Rhode is the main campaign city-state.

It is a dense extraction city with layered guild politics and heavy record control.
Its elected head is Governor Liliane FoxKilo, who also serves as council chairwoman and holds top-tier archive clearance.

## Inter-Colony Reality

City-states cooperate only when needed.
- They trade processed metals, tools, and specialists.
- They compete for contracts, routes, and influence.
- They weaponize information and supply access.

Officially they are partners in survival.
Operationally they are rival firms sharing one dangerous moon.

## Emerging Rival Colony

A new colony ship has recently reached TerraLuna orbit and is preparing for surface operations.

Established city-states see this as both opportunity and threat:
- new labor and tooling contracts
- new claims disputes
- new political leverage in old conflicts

This arrival gives the campaign a live external pressure beyond New-Rhode's internal crisis.
