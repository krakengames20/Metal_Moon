# 5 - Opening Scene

The opening takes place in New-Rhode during a public address by Governor Liliane FoxKilo.

Liliane speaks about colony unity, production targets, and the path forward. She references her own pod-named heritage as proof of the colony's capacity for renewal — the descendant of condemned families standing at the highest podium in New-Rhode.

Before she can finish, Alastair attacks her.

The shooting triggers panic, emergency lockdown, rapid Deployed Admin mobilization, and immediate political crisis inside the Administrative Hub.

The irony of the moment is invisible to the crowd: Alastair's target is not only the governor but the lie she embodies without knowing it. Her speech, intended as inspiration, is the very repetition of the fabrication that drove him to act.
