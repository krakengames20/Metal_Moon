- The foundational event is a Purge, not a lottery. Families were framed for treason (Leaver collusion) and exiled.
- Children were seized and declared Pupils of New-Rhode. Cryostasis was justified as mercy; the children functioned as hostages.
- Selection targeted the least productive 5% of families. The 5% threshold was a strategic calculation to avoid mass resistance.
- The Remainers lacked infrastructure for imprisonment or open violence; framing and exile was the only viable mechanism.
- The specific accusation: aiding the Leavers in organizing their departure and funneling resources.
- Exile was effectively a death sentence. Survivors formed TrailBlazer communities.
- The secret is not that the Purge happened — it is that the treason was fabricated.
- Liliane FoxKilo does not know the treason was fabricated. She is an unwitting instrument of the lie, publicly citing her family's condemnation as proof of New-Rhode's clemency.
- Alastair's trigger: a descendant of the purged families perpetuating the fabrication from the colony's highest office.
- Alastair's assassination attempt may be a deliberate ruse to lure a pursuit team outside the colony, where he can present evidence beyond KOR's interference.
