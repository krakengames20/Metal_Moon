# 0 - Setting

## TerraLuna

Metal Moon is set on TerraLuna, a giant metal moon colonized by corporate extraction teams.

The moon is hostile to human life:
- thin, metallic atmosphere
- extreme cold and dust storms
- unstable gravity events
- Rusties, native metal-eating lifeforms

The colonies were not founded as a utopia. They were founded as a profit project.

## Core Premise

Each major settlement operates like a corporate city-state. Every district exists to extract, process, and ship metal. Public life, law, and security are all tied to production targets.

People survive through guilds, contracts, and ration systems. Reputation can protect you; being outside the system usually kills you.

## Buried Mystery

The public history says the colony survived because loyal founders made hard but necessary choices.

Hidden evidence suggests the official story was rewritten.

The central mystery of the campaign is not just who attacked the governor. It is who wrote colony history, who benefited from it, and who paid the real cost.

## Biome Unknowns

TerraLuna is still poorly understood.
- Rusties behave in coordinated patterns that do not fully match known ecology.
- Some regions show anomalies that mining science cannot yet explain.
- Founder-era survey records are incomplete or redacted.

The moon may not be as inert as the original corporate reports claimed.
