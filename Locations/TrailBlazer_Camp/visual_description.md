# TrailBlazer Camp - Visual Description

## Overview

Hive-like settlement carved into canyon walls. Home to TrailBlazer communities — desert survivors who established permanent footholds in the harsh outer regions.

## Visual Elements

**Architecture**:
- Brutalist metal architecture with geometric patterns
- Interconnected tunnels carved into living rock
- Platforms and walkways connecting different levels
- Living spaces integrated into natural formations

**Aesthetic**:
- Industrial practical design
- TrailBlazer dust-camouflage patterns
- Organic growth pattern (hive-like structure)
- Vertical construction utilizing cliff faces

**Atmosphere**:
- Inhabited and lived-in feel
- Desert survivalist aesthetic
- Community built into harsh environment
- Functional over decorative

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Desert Sand and Oxidized Copper**
