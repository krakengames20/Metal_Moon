# Convoy & Barnabus - Visual Description

## Overview

The mechanized convoy used to pursue Alistair across the metal desert. Five Mules — four standard, one improvised legend.

## The Standard Mules: Katie, Sally, Lilly, Abby

**Design**:
- Big pressure wheels for metallic dust and rough terrain
- Tall fiberglass masks (windshields/dust shields) on the front
- Reflective foil solar canopies that fold down over the back
- Utilitarian, functional, built for endurance not speed
- Rock violently over uneven terrain

## Barnabus — The Ugly Duckling

**Design**:
- Older, heavier, scarred — a machine that should not still exist
- Improvised armor plating welded over half its frame
- Mismatched panels, welds visible, patched and re-patched
- Old analog needles twitching beside digital screens inside
- Hums like a buried reactor
- On-board fission reactor, solar panels for battery top-up and shade
- Engineering rig, experimental and fabrication setup
- Marion's workshop on wheels

**Character**:
- "Immortal and spiteful" — outlived everything designed to replace it
- Engineers Guild says it shouldn't exist
- The ugly duckling of the convoy, but the most capable
- A Frankenstein machine held together by Marion's obsessive maintenance

**Interior**:
- Cramped, cluttered with tools and spare parts
- Mixed analog and digital instrumentation
- Warm mechanical hum throughout
- Smells of engine oil and ozone

## Mood

A fleet of working beasts and one stubborn mechanical soul. Barnabus is not just a vehicle — it's Marion's identity externalized in metal.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Welded Steel, Rust Patch, and Solar Foil Silver**
