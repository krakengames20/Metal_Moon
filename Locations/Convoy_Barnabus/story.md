# Convoy & Barnabus

The convoy consists of five Mules: Katie, Sally, Lilly, Abby, and Barnabus.

Garvin rides in Katie with Cass. Marion and Tanya ride in Barnabus. The standard Mules carry supplies and security crates.

Barnabus is Marion's personal Mule — an old engineering rig she has constantly tinkered with and adapted. It carries a fission reactor, fabrication tools, and experimental equipment. It serves as the convoy's mobile workshop.
