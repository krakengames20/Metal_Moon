# ADMINISTRATIVE HUB
## New-Rhode's Government Center and Council Chambers

**Location Type**: Government facility, secure location, political power center
**District**: Government Quarter, New-Rhode (adjacent to Central Spoke Plaza)
**Significance**: Emergency Council meeting site, restricted archive location, political nerve center

---

## PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION

**Exterior**:
- **Structure**: 6-story building, reinforced rhodium-titanium alloy construction
- **Architecture**: Founder-era design (70+ years old but well-maintained)
- **Footprint**: 150 ft x 200 ft, imposing and utilitarian
- **Security**: Deployed Admin checkpoint at main entrance, keycard access to upper floors
- **Defenses**: Blast-resistant walls, gravity-stabilization system (GraviMancer-maintained)

**Interior Layout**:

**Ground Floor**:
- Public Records Hall (citizen services, permits, basic documentation)
- Deployed Admin Security Station (10-15 guards on shift)
- Emergency medical station (first aid, trauma stabilization)

**Second Floor**:
- Desk Admin offices (bureaucratic processing, scheduling)
- Resource allocation office (ration distribution, quota management)
- Guild liaison rooms (where representatives meet)

**Third Floor**:
- Council Chambers (Session 1 meeting location)
- Governor's Office (Liliane's workspace)
- Conference rooms (policy development, negotiations)

**Fourth Floor**:
- Classified Archives (restricted access, historical records)
- Data Analysis Center (monitoring, intelligence)
- IT infrastructure (servers, communication hub)

**Fifth Floor**:
- High-security storage (evidence, sensitive materials)
- Council members' private offices
- Secure communication center

**Sixth Floor**:
- Emergency command center (crisis management)
- Rooftop access (evacuation point, helipads for emergency transport if needed)
- Gravity event observation deck

**Basement Levels** (two sub-levels):
- Level B1: Staff facilities (break rooms, storage, maintenance)
- Level B2: Secure vault (founder artifacts, sealed records, emergency supplies)
- [[Hidden: Secret access to Marcus Kaine's private vault—Session 9 discovery]]

## TACTICAL INFORMATION

**Security Levels**:
- **Ground Floor**: Light security (public access, checkpoint only)
- **Floors 2-3**: Moderate (guild members and officials)
- **Floors 4-6**: Heavy (restricted access, biometrics required)
- **Basement**: Maximum (vault-level security)

**Access Control**:
- **Authorized Personnel**: Council members, senior guild reps, Governor
- **Restricted**: Classified Archives (Floor 4), High-Security Storage (Floor 5)
- **Forbidden**: Basement Vault (Council approval required)

**Infiltration Challenges**:
- **Social Engineering**: Persuasion DC 15 to gain floor access (pretend to belong)
- **Stealth**: Difficult (cameras, patrols); DC 16 to move undetected
- **Technology**: Hacking biometric locks DC 18 (requires tools and time)
- **Combat**: 10-15 Deployed Admins on site at any time (reinforcements arrive in 1d4 minutes)

**Escape Routes**:
- Main entrance (obvious, guarded)
- Maintenance stairwells (monitored but less guarded)
- Rooftop (risky—exposed, 6-story drop)
- Basement tunnels (connect to city infrastructure) [[May connect to Alastair's escape route]]

## SECURITY DETAILS (For Infiltration)

**Guard Patrols**:
- Ground Floor: 3 guards at checkpoint, 2 roaming
- Floors 2-3: 1 guard per floor (predictable routes)
- Floor 4: 1 dedicated guard at Archive entrance
- Floor 5: 2 guards (rotating shifts)
- Basement: 1 guard (B1 only; B2 is "sealed")

**Camera Coverage**:
- All hallways monitored
- Blind spots: Maintenance closets, stairwell corners
- Cameras not in Council Chambers (privacy/security)
- Archive cameras focus on entrance, not interior (budget/design limitation)

**Alarm Systems**:
- Doors log all access (digital record)
- Biometric failures trigger silent alert (security investigates)
- Archive breach = building lockdown
- Basement vault = armed response team

**Weaknesses** (for clever PCs):
- Staff badge cloning (Technology DC 16)
- Maintenance disguise (Deception DC 14 with appropriate gear)
- Council member impersonation (very risky, DC 20)
- Night shift has fewer guards (only 5 total building-wide)

## DESCRIPTIVE READ-ALOUDS

**Entering Building** (first impression):
> "The Administrative Hub looms before you, a testament to founder pragmatism. Six stories of rhodium-reinforced metal, windows like narrow eyes watching the street. Deployed Admins flank the entrance, hands near weapons. Inside, harsh lighting strips away shadows. Every surface reflects, giving the sensation of being watched from all angles. This is power's home. You are guests at best, intruders at worst."

**Council Chambers** (briefing scene):
> "The Council table gleams under clinical lights—polished rhodium reflecting three faces: Tarus Blackforge's scarred pragmatism, Syl Voidwalker's calculating stillness, Kess Brightshield's troubled conscience. Between them lies evidence: a blood-stained manifesto, a terrain scanner tracking Alastair's flight, the weight of a city's fear. They look at you not as heroes, but as tools. Useful. For now."

**Archives** (Session 2+, if accessed):
> "Row upon row of sealed cases stretch into shadow. Founder-era records, preserved in TerraLuna's cold. But something's wrong. Gaps in the filing system. Corrupted database entries. Someone's been here, digging through history like a surgeon excising tumors. What truth was cut away? And why?"

**Vault Discovery** (Session 9):
> "Beneath the basement, where maps say only foundation exists, you find it: a hidden level. Marcus Kaine's personal vault, sealed for sixty years. The lock bears his biometric signature—long dead, but his daughter's DNA might suffice. Inside, if you dare: the architect's confession. The truth that could shatter New-Rhode. The choice that destroyed a man's soul."
