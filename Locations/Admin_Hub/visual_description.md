# Administrative Hub - Visual Description

## Overview

New-Rhode's administrative center. The bureaucratic heart of TerraLuna's colonial government.

## Visual Elements

**Architecture**:
- Brutalist government buildings
- Rhodium accents throughout (signifying authority)
- Cold institutional aesthetic
- Metal walls with bureaucratic efficiency

**Interior/Exterior**:
- Both exterior facades and interior corridors relevant
- Sterile and controlled environment
- Harsh artificial lighting
- Geometric precision in design

**Atmosphere**:
- Cold and impersonal
- Authority and control
- Bureaucratic power made physical
- Contrast to organic TrailBlazer settlements

## Mood

Power, control, institutional authority. The machine of government made manifest in architecture.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Rhodium Silver and Cold Blue**
