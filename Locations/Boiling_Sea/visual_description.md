# Boiling Sea - Visual Description

## Overview

A vast shallow geothermal basin west of the dust desert — a natural fission zone with a thin crust over superheated ground.

## Visual Elements

**Terrain**:
- Shallow steaming basin stretching impossibly wide
- Yellow mineral crust over unstable ground
- Heat shimmer distorting the horizon
- Descending footprints visible in the crust

**Atmosphere**:
- Warm vapor rising constantly
- Steam rolling upward in thick columns
- Hot ground contrasting with cold sky overhead
- Radiation pockets invisible but lethal
- Beautiful but deadly — an alien thermal wasteland

**Lighting**:
- Sunset light filtering through steam
- Warm golden-orange glow from below mixing with cold sky above
- Silhouettes of figures and vehicles on the ridge edge

**Key Visual**:
- Small convoy parked at ridge edge
- Steam rising from the basin below
- Vast dead desert ahead beyond the steam
- Alistair's footprints descending straight into the basin without hesitation

## Mood

Awe and dread. A natural barrier that should be impassable — and someone walked straight through it. The sublime and the lethal fused.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Sulfur Yellow, Steam White, and Fission Orange**
