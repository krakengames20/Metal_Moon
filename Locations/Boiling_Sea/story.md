# Boiling Sea

A natural fission basin west of the dust desert. Hot ground, radiation pockets, bad footing. Garvin calls it ugly death.

Alistair's tracks descend straight into it without hesitation — suggesting either madness or knowledge of a safe path.

Scene 5 of Act 1 takes place on the ridge overlooking the Boiling Sea at sunset. The convoy camps here before attempting a crossing at first light.
