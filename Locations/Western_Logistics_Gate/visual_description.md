# Western Logistics Gate - Visual Description

## Overview

New-Rhode's western blast gate — the heavy industrial exit point where the colony ends and the metal desert begins.

## Visual Elements

**Architecture**:
- Massive grinding blast gates, industrial steel
- Sealed acid-runoff channels beneath heavy steel decking
- Thick concrete foundation and walls
- Drone towers flanking the corridor

**Terrain**:
- Fine metallic dust skating in silver streams across concrete
- Acid runoff trickling through sealed channels
- Transition zone: colony infrastructure giving way to grey plains

**Beyond the Gate**:
- Grey plains, broken ridges, pale stone
- Endless metallic desert stretching west
- Cold sulfur morning light

**Atmosphere**:
- Cold sulfur dawn air
- Industrial threshold between civilization and wilderness
- The last safe ground before the unknown
- Heavy, utilitarian, no beauty — pure function

## Mood

Departure. The point of no return. Colony safety ending in steel and concrete, the desert beginning in dust and silence.

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Industrial Steel and Sulfur Yellow**
