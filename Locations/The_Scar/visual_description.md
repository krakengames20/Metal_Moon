# The Scar - Visual Description

## Overview

Massive canyon carved through TerraLuna's metal crust. A geological marvel and reminder of ancient catastrophic impacts.

## Visual Elements

**Scale**: Monumental; cliffs dwarf human figures

**Cliff Walls**:
- Towering metallic walls showing geometric Widmanstatten-like patterns
- Cross-hatched metal etching visible throughout
- Ancient meteoritic iron structure exposed

**Crystals**:
- Pallasite olivine crystals embedded in walls
- Glow amber when backlit by TerraLuna's sun
- Range from small clusters to massive formations

**Atmosphere**:
- Deep shadows in canyon depths
- Dramatic lighting from olivine glow
- Sense of overwhelming scale and geological time

## Color Accent for Artwork

**Amber Olivine and Rust Brown**
